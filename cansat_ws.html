CanSat Workshop

A CanSat is a simulation of a real satellite, integrated within the volume and shape of an aerodynamic box. The challenge for the students is to fit all the major subsystems such as power, sensors, communication system, etc. into this minimal volume.

Schedule for the day:
  • Theory about rocket and its flight dynamics
  • Design & fabrication of rocket and its frame
  • Design & fabrication of parachute
  • Design & assembly of payload (Arduino, Sensors, PCB, ...)
  • Programming the onboard computer
  • Testing & calibration of sensors
  • Satellite launch!
