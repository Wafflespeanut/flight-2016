Who are we?

We, "Majestians" are the members of the Association of Aeronautical Engineers (AAE).

Pioneering in the field of Aviation in India, MIT was the first technical instituition to provide formal education in the field of aerospace. It's the birthplace of some of the best Aerospace engineers, including late former President of India, Dr. APJ .Abdul Kalam.

Continuing the trend, MIT continues to produce quality engineers who contribute to the development of Aeronautics.

What we do?

Every year, we take pride in organizing a national-level intercollegiate technical symposium, "Flight", which provides a launch pad for aspiring aerospace engineers in India.

We welcome fellow students from all over the country for highly profession technical events in the Aerospace domain. Welcome onboard our "Flight". We hope you have a pleasant ride!
