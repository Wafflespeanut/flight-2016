UAV Flight Controller
This workshop is about designing, developing and integrating a flight controller into an UAV. We will be focusing on the selection of microcontroller, along with the integration of barometer, gyroscope & accelerometer (and optional GPS) into the flight controller, which can finally perform a semi- (or fully) autonomous flight required for an UAV.
